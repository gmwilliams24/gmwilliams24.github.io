Econ Parody Video

This is an economics parody music video based off “Just The Two Of Us” by Bill Withers and Grover Washington, Jr. We created this parody video for the Arizona Council on Economics Education competition. Competing against high schoolers across the state, we managed to make the top four nominees but sadly didn’t win first (although we totally should have). I made this video with a group of friends and despite some difficulties, it was really fun.
